ECC006 Homework Assignment #10: E-Commerce Popularity Survey

Survey Methodology

I surveyed 20 people from among my friends, family members, coworkers, and classmates to determine their e-commerce habits. The survey covered purchasing behavior, shopping frequency, demographic information, and preferred shopping sites.

Survey Results

a. Online Purchasing Behavior

  • 85% (17 people) have purchased an item online
  • 15% (3 people) have shopped but not purchased online

b. Purchase Frequency

  • 20% (4 people) purchase online once a week
  • 45% (9 people) purchase online once a month
  • 20% (4 people) purchase online once a year

Analysis and Conclusions

The survey results clearly demonstrate the widespread adoption of e-commerce, with 85% of respondents having made online purchases. The data reveals several key patterns:

  • Younger demographics (ages 18-39) account for 75% of online shoppers in this survey
  • Monthly online shopping is the most common frequency (45% of respondents)
  • Amazon dominates as the preferred shopping site, mentioned by 60% of respondents
  • Education levels show a correlation with online shopping, with college-educated individuals being the most active

These findings suggest that e-commerce has become a mainstream shopping channel, particularly among younger, educated demographics, with Amazon maintaining significant market leadership.

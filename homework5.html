Amazon vs. Alibaba Design Analysis:

  • Repetition: Both use consistent navigation menus and color schemes throughout
  • Contrast: Amazon uses yellow/orange against blue, Alibaba uses orange against white
  • Proximity: Related products grouped together on both sites
  • Alignment: Grid layouts with left-aligned product listings
